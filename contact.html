Common questions
Do you work with startups?

It depends on the stage and the problem. The Playbook is a good entry point for early-stage companies who need direction before committing to ongoing strategy. If the budget doesn't yet match the ambition, I'll tell you that honestly — and we can talk about when the timing is right.

Do you do production?

No. Mission Over Metrics is a strategy-only practice. I work with your existing production resources — or help you find the right production partner once we have a clear brief. A great strategy brief makes production better and cheaper. That's the point.

How quickly can you start?

Typically within two to three weeks of our first call, depending on current availability. The Playbook takes three to four weeks from kickoff to delivery. The Partnership and Engine Room engagements can be structured to start faster if there's urgency. Ask on the call and we'll find the right timing.

What do you need from us?

An honest conversation about what's working and what isn't. Access to anyone who has context on the business objectives and the customer. And a genuine commitment to starting with why — not defending existing content decisions. The process only works if we're honest with each other.
